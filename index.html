About Me

I am a licensed physician and Post-Doc researcher leading the Gastrointestinal Artificial Intelligence Network (GAIN). My work focuses on integrating artificial intelligence into clinical gastroenterology, specifically for Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD)[cite: 15].

Current research involves the development of deep learning models for endoscopic severity scoring in Ulcerative Colitis and Crohn's Disease[cite: 95, 120]. I also utilize Danish nationwide registry data to study disease epidemiology and treatment outcomes[cite: 88, 108].
